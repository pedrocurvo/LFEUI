We started by discussing the samples that we wil be using during the day.
In the previous session we had already concluded that there would be two different types of samples: glass samples with lithium in their composition and also implanted samples. The later consists of a thin layer of lithium implanted in a silicon substrate.
As for the glass samples, they are not available and we will simply use three different samples, each one with a different substance/material whose composition is known and varies from sample to sample.
